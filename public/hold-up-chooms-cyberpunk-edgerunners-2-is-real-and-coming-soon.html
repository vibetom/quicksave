Hold Up, Chooms: Cyberpunk: Edgerunners 2 is Real and Coming Soon

Get ready to dive back into Night City with a brand-new crew, as the critically acclaimed anime confirms its return in Autumn 2026.

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jun 29, 2026 · 19:21 UTC 2 MIN

Night City is calling, chooms, and it's not just V on the line this time. We can officially confirm that Cyberpunk: Edgerunners 2 is slated for an Autumn 2026 release, a piece of news that has the QUICKSAVE desk absolutely buzzing. Fans who fell in love with the original series and the sprawling world of Cyberpunk 2077 now have a definitive date to mark on their calendars.

This isn't a direct continuation of David and Lucy's saga, however. The new season will carve its own path, introducing a completely fresh cast of characters. They'll be navigating a brand-new story, but rest assured, the entire narrative will unfold within the familiar, neon-drenched confines of Night City. It ’s a smart move, allowing for new narratives to flourish without treading on established ground.

Driving this highly anticipated return is a powerhouse collaboration. The announcement came directly from the combined forces of CD Projekt Red, Studio Trigger, and Netflix. This partnership signals a commitment to maintaining the high bar for quality and kinetic style that made the first season a runaway success.

If you're already itching for a glimpse, you're in luck. A "bloodthirsty trailer" has already been released, looking "amazing" and offering a tantalizing first look at the action and style to expect from this new chapter.
