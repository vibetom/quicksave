Game Pass July 2026: More Than Just Palworld, But Also Palworld

A closer look at a stacked month for Xbox Game Pass subscribers, featuring a highly anticipated creature-collector's 1.0 release, a classic Halo remake, and a dark fantasy Metroidvania that's already earning buzz.

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jul 07, 2026 · 11:23 UTC 3 MIN

Xbox Game Pass subscribers are looking at a particularly strong July 2026, with a lineup that blends highly anticipated updates, a significant remake, and compelling indie experiences. While the full release of Palworld 1.0 is undoubtedly drawing much of the attention, the service is also delivering a fresh take on a genre-defining shooter and a critically acclaimed Metroidvania.

The much-anticipated Palworld 1.0 is set to fully launch on July 10, promising a substantial expansion for fans of the creature-collecting survival game. This major update will introduce a brand-new endgame area, expand the game's narrative with new story and lore elements, bring in fresh Pals to discover, and rework the progression system for a richer experience.

Adding another layer of excitement, Halo: Campaign Evolved is slated for release on July 28. This ambitious project reworks the original Xbox title from the ground up using Unreal Engine 5, promising significantly improved graphics. The remake goes beyond a visual overhaul, incorporating three new prequel missions that offer fresh insights into the classic universe.

Kicking off the month's major additions, Winds of Arcana: Ruination joined Game Pass on July 6. This dark fantasy Metroidvania, which previously made its debut as a PC exclusive in November 2025, brings a rich, intricate world to console players. Its addition highlights Game Pass's consistent effort to offer a diverse range of titles, from indie gems to blockbuster experiences.

Beyond these marquee titles, July's Game Pass lineup is rounded out with several other notable additions. Subscribers can already dive into Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 3+4, which landed on July 2, bringing classic skateboarding action. Later in the month, Ascend to ZERO will arrive on July 13, followed by the crafting and exploration title The Planet Crafter on July 21. This varied collection ensures there's something for every type of player as the summer progresses.
