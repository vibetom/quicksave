GTA 6 Pre-Orders Are Live, But Don't Get Got By Scammers

Everything you need to know about legitimate GTA 6 pre-orders, confirmed editions, and how to spot the fake 'early access' offers flooding the internet.

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jun 24, 2026 · 11:22 UTC 2 MIN

The wait is officially over for the initial step toward Grand Theft Auto 6. Pre-orders for Rockstar's highly anticipated title begin at midnight local time on June 25, 2026, setting the stage for its eventual launch on November 19, 2026.

Players looking to secure their copy will find that Rockstar has confirmed both bonus content for early buyers and the specifics for an 'Ultimate Edition' of the game. The confirmed price for GTA 6 will also deliver a dedicated single-player experience at launch. However, a notable detail for physical collectors: physical copies of GTA 6 will not include a disc, instead packaging a code in a box.

While legitimate pre-orders are now in motion, the hype surrounding GTA 6 has become a fertile ground for scammers. Reports indicate that promises of early entry to GTA 6 are unequivocally scams. Rockstar Games has not authorized any early access programs, a crucial detail for anyone encountering such claims.

These fraudulent schemes often manifest as scam websites promoting 'VIP early access' to GTA 6, frequently demanding hundreds of dollars in cryptocurrency. To be clear: any online destination promoting early access, unique downloads, or playable iterations before the official November 19, 2026 release date is fraudulent. Gaming news outlets are strongly advising players to acquire pre-orders exclusively through official retail channels to avoid falling victim to these pervasive scams.

As the industry collectively holds its breath for GTA 6's release, with other publishers reportedly avoiding November launch dates to steer clear of the looming competition, it's more important than ever for players to exercise caution and verify sources. Stay vigilant and ensure your pre-order comes directly from authorized channels.
