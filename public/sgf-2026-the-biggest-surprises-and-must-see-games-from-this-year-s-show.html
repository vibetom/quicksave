SGF 2026: The Biggest Surprises and Must-See Games From This Year's Show

From *Final Fantasy VII Revelation* to *Resident Evil Veronica* and a new *Alien: Isolation*, here's everything you need to know about the major reveals and what's coming next.

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jun 19, 2026 · 21:43 UTC 4 MIN

Summer Game Fest 2026, helmed by Geoff Keighley, just wrapped up, leaving a substantial trail of reveals, new trailers, and updates that promise to shape the gaming landscape for the coming year and beyond. This year's showcase proved surprisingly robust, delivering a lineup rich with highly anticipated sequels and remakes that signal a strong future for established franchises across diverse genres.

The most colossal announcement undoubtedly arrived with Final Fantasy VII Revelation, officially confirming Part 3 of the Final Fantasy VII Remake saga. This monumental conclusion is slated for a Spring 2027 launch, arriving simultaneously on all platforms, ensuring no fan is left behind in Cloud's continuing journey.

Survival horror carved out a significant presence at the Fest. We saw the official unveiling of Resident Evil Veronica as a remake. Despite its initial first-person announcement trailer, it was confirmed as a third-person experience, returning to a classic Resident Evil perspective. Adding to the tension, Alien: Isolation 2 was also announced. Creative Assembly showcased a new trailer for the title, confirming a new setting: a station located on a planet, ready to deliver fresh scares.

Fan favorites are making a strong comeback. The long-awaited The Wolf Among Us 2 received a release window, set for 2027, giving fans of the Fables universe something to look forward to. To bridge the gap, a remaster of the original The Wolf Among Us is set to arrive for Holiday 2026. Meanwhile, Mafia: The Old Country will receive an expansion titled Man of Honor, scheduled to launch on August 14, 2026, extending the gritty narrative for loyal players.

The event also brought some unexpected twists and new horizons. Virtua Fighter Crossroads was unveiled, taking the iconic fighting series in a bold new direction as an open-world brawler. Developed by RGG Studio, this intriguing title is aiming for a 2027 release. Bandai Namco also announced Gundam Rogue Orbit, a fast-paced action game set within a brand-new Gundam timeline, also launching in 2027, promising high-octane mech combat.

While many major titles are set for 2027, players won't have to wait that long for every announced game. Palworld 1.0 is officially set to release on July 10, bringing the full version of the creature-collecting phenomenon. Additionally, a sequel, Lords of the Fallen 2, is scheduled to debut on Steam this Fall, offering a new challenge for action-RPG enthusiasts. Looking slightly further ahead, Monster Hunter Wilds will expand its world with the Ascendance expansion in 2027, ensuring the hunt continues.
