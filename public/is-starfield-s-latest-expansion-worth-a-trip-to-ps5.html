Is Starfield's Latest Expansion Worth a Trip to PS5?

Shattered Space just landed on PlayStation 5, bringing a new story and a 'car mech system.' Here's what new players and returning fans can expect.

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jun 21, 2026 · 21:51 UTC 3 MIN

Bethesda's sprawling space opera, Starfield, has finally touched down on PlayStation 5, bringing with it the first major expansion, Shattered Space. This simultaneous launch on April 7, 2026, marks a significant moment for the title, throwing open its vast universe to a brand-new audience while setting the stage for Bethesda's ambitious long-term plans.

For those venturing into the cosmos on PS5, Shattered Space offers a fresh narrative chapter. Players will find themselves answering a distress signal, which guides them to Va'ruun'kai, the ancestral home world of House Va'ruun. This marks the first time players can explore this 'completely handcrafted' location, where a massive vortex has ravaged much of the city of Dazra, plunging House Va'ruun into turmoil. The mission: uncover the long-held secrets surrounding the followers of the Great Serpent.

Beyond the narrative, the expansion also introduces a new 'car mech system,' designed to seamlessly integrate with the game's existing mechanics and aesthetic. Bethesda Game Studios has emphasized enhancing the core gameplay and maintaining consistency, which suggests these new elements are built to feel like a natural extension of the Starfield experience.

This expansion is just the beginning, according to creative director Todd Howard, who confirmed Bethesda Game Studios intends to release a story expansion for Starfield every year. This commitment paints a picture of a constantly evolving universe, with new narratives and gameplay layers added regularly, keeping the journey fresh for explorers across all platforms.

However, it's worth noting the initial journey for Shattered Space on its September 30, 2024, release for Windows and Xbox Series X/S was met with mixed to negative critical and user reception, as reported by Wikipedia. New and returning players on PlayStation 5 will now have the chance to experience this chapter for themselves.

Whether you're picking up Starfield for the first time on PS5 or diving back in, Shattered Space is included in the Starfield Premium Edition, Premium Edition Upgrade, and the Constellation Edition, ensuring immediate access to this new adventure and the promise of more to come.
