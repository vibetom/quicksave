What You Missed (And Can Still Catch) In Esports World Cup's Electrifying Week 3

Paris is buzzing as the Esports World Cup 2026 hits its stride, with champions crowned, millions on the line, and viewership soaring. Here's your breakdown of the games heating up the stage.

The Esports World Cup (EWC) 2026 is currently underway in Paris, France, transforming the city into a global hub for competitive gaming. Running from July 6 to August 23, 2026, this event marks a significant new chapter for the esports scene, drawing attention from across the globe.

As the tournament enters its third week, from July 20-26, the energy in Paris is palpable. This segment of the EWC 2026 is a whirlwind of high-stakes action, featuring major competitions in titles such as EA SPORTS FC, PUBG: BATTLEGROUNDS, the Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) Mid Season Cup, and Teamfight Tactics, as reported by DAZN News US.

At the heart of this global spectacle is a staggering prize pool. The EWC 2026 boasts a record-breaking $75 million in total prize money, with an additional $30 million dedicated to the Club Championship. This immense financial incentive positions the event as the largest tournament in the history of esports, according to Globe PR Wire (via Esports World Cup 2026).

Fans of virtual football have a particular reason to tune in this week. The EA SPORTS FC Pro World Championship is set to crown its champion on July 25-26. Adding a touch of celebrity flair, football legend Karim Benzema is scheduled to participate in a showmatch, further elevating the event's profile, as noted by DAZN News US.

The momentum building around the EWC 2026 is undeniable. Esports Charts reported that Week 2 of the event generated over 47.8 million Hours Watched. This viewership figure not only doubled that of the opening week but also surpassed Week 2 of the EWC 2025 by a significant 34.4%, indicating a substantial surge in audience engagement.

The Esports World Cup 2026 is making a powerful statement about its global ambitions, successfully attracting both players and viewers with its massive prize pool and diverse lineup of games. Week 3 stands as a microcosm of this broader success, showcasing intense competition, popular titles, and even a celebrity crossover, all contributing to an electrifying atmosphere.
