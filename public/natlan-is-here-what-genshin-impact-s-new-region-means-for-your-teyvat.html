Natlan is Here: What Genshin Impact's New Region Means For Your Teyvat Journey

Version 5.0 brings dragons, new characters, and a boatload of anniversary goodies, making it the perfect time to explore the Nation of Pyro.

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jun 18, 2026 · 17:42 UTC 5 MIN

The latest major update for _Genshin Impact_, Version 5.0, known as "Flowers Resplendent on the Sun-Scorched Sojourn," became available on August 28, 2024, revealing the long-anticipated Nation of Pyro, Natlan. This isn't just another patch; it's a colossal content drop that redefines exploration and offers a golden invitation for both seasoned adventurers and fresh faces to dive into Teyvat.

Natlan, the sixth major nation, is a fiery new frontier now open for exploration. While the full scope of this region will undoubtedly unfold over time, players can immediately immerse themselves by interacting with three of its six distinct tribes, hinting at a rich tapestry of stories yet to be told.

Beyond new lands, Version 5.0 introduces groundbreaking exploration mechanics. Imagine soaring through canyons or burrowing under mountainsides as one of Natlan's formidable Saurians. Players can transform into creatures like the Tepetlisaurus, Yumkasaurus, and Koholasaurus, each boasting unique abilities designed to reshape how you traverse the world. For instance, the Tepetlisaurus offers the power to burrow and scale sheer cliffs, unlocking previously inaccessible pathways and secrets.

The update also brings a fresh roster of characters to recruit. Three new faces hail from Natlan: Mualani, a 5-star Hydro character; Kinich, a 5-star Dendro character; and Kachina, a new 4-star Geo character who will be given away for free. The initial banner phase, running from August 28 to September 17, features Mualani alongside a rerun for the fan-favorite Kazuha, with Kachina, Xinyan, and Bennett rounding out the 4-star options. Following that, from September 17 to October 8, Kinich will take the spotlight with a rerun for the powerful Raiden Shogun.

This isn't just a new chapter; it's a celebration. Version 5.0 marks _Genshin Impact_'s fourth anniversary, and HoYoverse is rolling out the red carpet with generous rewards. Players can look forward to acquiring a free 5-star character chosen from the standard wish pool, up to 10 Intertwined Fates, a healthy 1,600 Primogems, and a collection of exclusive gadgets. This makes the present moment an exceptionally appealing time for returning players or those considering their first steps into Teyvat.

Accessibility is a key focus. Early and mid-game players will find a clear path to Natlan: simply completing all Archon Quests in Mondstadt and reaching Adventure Rank 19 will unlock Teleport Waypoints, while those at Adventure Rank 28 who have cleared all Liyue Archon Quests can jump straight into Natlan's main story. Pre-installation was available across PC and mobile platforms, with PlayStation users experiencing automatic downloads. The total size of the update files varies from 2.5 gigabytes to 73 gigabytes, depending on the specific platform.

Finally, the update delivers a suite of quality-of-life enhancements that polish the overall experience. Expect an improved Adventurer Handbook, a handy new enhancement progression calculator to streamline character development, and persistent artifact filters, making inventory management a smoother affair. These systemic improvements, coupled with the rich new content and anniversary bonuses, cement Version 5.0 as a landmark update for _Genshin Impact_.
