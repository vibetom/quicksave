The Lands Between Just Got Bigger: What to Know About Elden Ring's 'Shadow of the Erdtree' DLC

From its massive new realm to its surprising Game of the Year nod, here's everything you need to know about the long-awaited expansion.

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jun 25, 2026 · 13:33 UTC 3 MIN

The wait is over, Tarnished: Shadow of the Erdtree, the highly anticipated downloadable content for Elden Ring, officially descended upon the gaming world on June 20 and June 21, 2024. This sprawling expansion, initially announced back in February 2023, is set to significantly broaden the already immense scope of the Lands Between.

Players who've been yearning for more challenges, secrets, and lore will find their wishes granted. Shadow of the Erdtree ushers in an entirely new realm, populated with fresh faces, formidable bosses, and an arsenal of new weapons and spells to uncover. It's an ambitious addition, promising to pull players back into FromSoftware's signature blend of brutal beauty and intricate world-building.

What truly sets this expansion apart is its unprecedented recognition. Shadow of the Erdtree stands as the first DLC in history to receive a nomination for a Game of the Year Award. This nod speaks volumes about the quality and impact the expansion delivers, elevating it beyond the typical add-on content.

For those eager to dive into the new content, a word of caution: the expansion requires the base game, Elden Ring, to run. Once that's sorted, players have a few options for acquiring the DLC. The standard edition is available for $39.99 USD, with a Premium Bundle offering additional content at $49.99 USD. For the most dedicated collectors, a Physical Collector's Edition is also available for $249.99 USD.

However, the path to the Shadow Realm wasn't entirely smooth for everyone. While PlayStation users enjoyed the convenience of preloading the DLC 48 hours ahead of launch, players on Xbox and PC had to wait until release day to begin their downloads. Regardless of when the download began, the journey into Shadow of the Erdtree promises to be another epic chapter in the Elden Ring saga, filled with the peril and discovery fans have come to expect.
