Turns Out Everyone Wanted More Elden Ring: Shadow of the Erdtree Smashes Sales Records

FromSoftware's latest expansion sold over 5 million copies in its first week and continues to rack up critical praise, proving its difficulty is no deterrent for eager Tarnished.

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jul 20, 2026 · 21:39 UTC 3 MIN

FromSoftware's latest journey into the Lands Between, Elden Ring: Shadow of the Erdtree, has quickly become a titan in its own right, selling over 5 million units globally within its first week. This grand total spans across consoles (PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Xbox Series X|S, and Xbox One) and personal computers through Steam. Bandai Namco Europe confirmed these impressive figures on July 19, 2026, just weeks after the expansion's initial launch on June 21, 2026.

The expansion's rapid ascent is built on the colossal foundation of the base game, Elden Ring, which had already moved over 25 million units worldwide by June 2026. This pre-existing goodwill and massive player base undoubtedly fueled the intense anticipation for additional content, translating directly into the expansion's swift commercial success.

Beyond its sales prowess, Shadow of the Erdtree has also garnered widespread critical adoration. The expansion currently holds a coveted "universal acclaim" status on Metacritic. The PlayStation 5 version boasts a score of 92 based on 94 critic reviews, while the PC version sits at a 93 from 32 reviews. The Xbox Series X version leads the pack with a 94, based on 10 critic reviews. These scores reflect a consistent chorus of praise for the new content.

Among the many discussions surrounding the expansion, its formidable difficulty stands out. Many outlets are reporting on how Shadow of the Erdtree presents an exceptionally challenging experience, even for those seasoned Tarnished who have conquered the base game. Yet, rather than deterring players, this notorious difficulty seems to be a catalyst, sparking vibrant conversations and deeper engagement within the community.

With such rapid sales and top-tier critical reception, Shadow of the Erdtree is more than just a successful add-on; it's a defining moment for modern game expansions. It underscores the immense appetite players have for meticulously crafted, challenging content that expands beloved universes, proving that a well-executed vision can still capture the gaming world's imagination, even if it demands every ounce of their skill.
