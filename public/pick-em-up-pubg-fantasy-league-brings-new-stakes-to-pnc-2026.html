Pick 'Em Up: PUBG Fantasy League Brings New Stakes to PNC 2026

PUBG Esports just dropped a fantasy league for the PNC 2026, letting PC players draft teams and earn points based on real-world performance. Get your roster locked in before June 22!

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jun 21, 2026 · 18:58 UTC 2 MIN

The world of PUBG Esports is getting a fresh layer of strategy and engagement with the launch of PUBG Fantasy, a brand-new fantasy league that kicks off right alongside the PNC 2026 tournament. This new platform invites fans to move beyond passive spectating and dive into the action with their own carefully curated rosters.

Players in PUBG Fantasy build a roster by selecting from the professional teams competing in PNC 2026. The real-world performance of these chosen teams directly translates into Fantasy Points for participants, creating a tangible link between the live competition and a player's strategic choices. A clever twist in this system is how team prices dynamically shift based on how frequently they are picked by other players, demanding careful consideration and foresight in roster construction.

The roster creation period for PUBG Fantasy began on June 17th, following the conclusion of live server maintenance, and will be active until June 22nd at 08:00 UTC. The main event, PNC 2026, will then unfold from June 23rd to June 28th. Once the dust settles, a reward claim window will open on June 29th at 02:00 UTC, closing on July 7th at 00:00 UTC.

Currently, the PUBG Fantasy service is exclusively available for PC players. For those looking to deepen their involvement, an optional Event Pass: PNC 2026 is also on offer for $3.99 on PC. This pass allows players to earn XP by completing missions and simply surviving in-game, adding another dimension to the esports festivities.
