EWC League of Legends Playoffs Begin: Who's Left Standing?

Eight teams battle it out in a single-elimination bracket for their share of a $2 million prize pool, with the Grand Final just days away.

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jul 17, 2026 · 15:53 UTC 2 MIN

The Esports World Cup 2026 is shifting into high gear as the League of Legends playoffs officially kick off today, July 17. The remaining eight teams are about to plunge into a high-stakes, single-elimination knockout bracket, where every match determines their fate.

After a grueling Group Stage, only a select few have advanced. Notable teams like G2 Esports, Sentinels, GAM Esports, and MIBR.LOS have been eliminated, leaving an intense field to vie for the championship. According to Insider Gaming, the Western presence has significantly dwindled, with G2 and LYON out of the running. This leaves Karmine Corp as the solitary Western squad among the tournament's top contenders, who are largely Eastern powerhouses such as HLE and BLG.

The pace of these playoffs is set to be blistering. All Quarterfinal matches are scheduled for today, July 17, pushing teams to perform under immediate pressure. The Semifinals will follow swiftly tomorrow, July 18, leading directly into the Third Place Match and the Grand Final on Sunday, July 19. It’s a rapid-fire progression that promises a champion will be crowned in short order.

Adding to the intensity, the League of Legends EWC tournament boasts a substantial $2 million prize pool. This significant reward amplifies the stakes for every remaining team, turning each victory into a critical step towards financial success and competitive glory. With the bracket now set and the clock ticking, the next few days are poised to deliver a thrilling conclusion to this major esports event.
