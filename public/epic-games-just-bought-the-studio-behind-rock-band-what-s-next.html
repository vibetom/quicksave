Epic Games Just Bought the Studio Behind Rock Band. What's Next?

The creators of iconic rhythm games are joining Epic, promising 'musical journeys and gameplay for Fortnite' and more.

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jul 13, 2026 · 11:35 UTC 2 MIN

Epic Games has officially acquired Harmonix, the acclaimed studio that brought us era-defining rhythm games like Rock Band, Guitar Hero, and Dance Central. This union, announced by both Epic Games and Harmonix on their respective news channels, marks a significant convergence of gaming giants and musical pioneers.

Fans of Harmonix's current lineup needn't worry: the studio has confirmed it will continue to develop and support its existing portfolio, which includes the DJ mixing game Fuser and the enduring Rock Band 4. So, the beat goes on for these beloved titles.

However, the real crescendo of this acquisition lies in Harmonix's future projects with Epic. The newly integrated team will be focusing their considerable expertise on creating "musical journeys and gameplay for Fortnite." This points to a deeper, more interactive integration of music within Epic's expansive ecosystem, transforming Fortnite from a battle royale into an even richer platform for sonic experiences.

This strategic move by Epic Games underscores a growing ambition for music-driven experiences, expanding beyond traditional gaming genres. While the immediate focus for Harmonix will be on Fortnite, the integration of their seasoned team and their deep understanding of music-based gameplay suggests a broader vision. Some journalists, like those at The Verge, are speculating about the potential for Harmonix's musical prowess to influence other Epic properties, even suggesting a revitalization of music-based virtual reality experiences.

Though the financial details of the acquisition remain undisclosed, this deal clearly signals a substantial investment in interactive music content. It opens the door for new innovations within the rhythm game space, and for music to play an even more central role in the ever-evolving world of Epic Games.
