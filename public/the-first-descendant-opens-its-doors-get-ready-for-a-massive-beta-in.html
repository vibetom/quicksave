The First Descendant Opens Its Doors: Get Ready for a Massive Beta in Late 2026

Nexon's co-op looter shooter is gearing up for a widespread open beta across all major platforms, showcasing its flashy gameplay and stunning Unreal Engine 5 graphics.

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jul 11, 2026 · 18:13 UTC 3 MIN

Nexon has just pulled back the curtain on a significant next step for its cooperative, free-to-play treasure hunt shooter, _The First Descendant_. An open beta is officially scheduled for late 2026, setting the stage for a much broader audience to jump into the action.

This isn't the first time players will have a chance to try out _The First Descendant_. The game previously hosted a closed beta in October 2024, giving an early cohort a taste of its sci-fi world. The upcoming open beta, however, casts a wider net, inviting more players to join the fray.

Accessibility is key, as the beta will be available across a broad spectrum of platforms. Gamers will be able to experience the title on PC through Steam, current-generation consoles like PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S, as well as previous-generation hardware including PlayStation 4 and Xbox One, ensuring widespread participation in this testing phase.

The announcement arrived alongside a fresh trailer, offering a deeper look at what awaits. The footage highlighted core gameplay mechanics, including dynamic grapple hook movement, a diverse array of character abilities, and the promise of epic, large-scale boss battles that will put teamwork to the test.

Nexon is also putting a strong emphasis on the visual fidelity of _The First Descendant_. The game is powered by Unreal Engine 5, and the publisher has been keen to showcase its impressive graphics, aiming to deliver a visually striking experience for players.

This comprehensive open beta serves as a crucial milestone on the road to the game's official launch, which is anticipated in early 2027. It's an opportunity for Nexon to gather extensive player feedback and for a massive community to experience _The First Descendant_ before its full debut.
