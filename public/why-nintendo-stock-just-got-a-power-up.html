Why Nintendo Stock Just Got a Power-Up

Recent analyst recommendations, upcoming events, and strong financials are painting a bullish picture for the gaming titan.

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jul 20, 2026 · 19:12 UTC 4 MIN

Nintendo’s stock is on a noticeable upward climb, signaling a period of robust health and investor confidence for the gaming giant. On the US market, Nintendo (NTDOY) shares are trading at $11.07. Across the Pacific, the Tokyo Stock Exchange (TYO: 7974) saw Nintendo’s stock price hit 7,294.0 JPY, marking a 3.02% increase in just 24 hours.

This surge isn't just market noise; it's backed by a strong consensus from financial analysts. According to Robinhood data, a significant 70.4% of analysts are recommending a "Buy" for NTDOY, with another 25.9% advising to "Hold." This strong endorsement is buoyed by what analysts and investors are calling "numerous bullish events upcoming" that could propel the company to a new peak. The horizon for Nintendo appears bright, with several key catalysts potentially fueling further growth. This collaboration taps into one of Nintendo's most beloved series, promising fresh merchandise for fans and new revenue streams for the company.

Adding to the buzz are persistent reports swirling around a potential successor to the incredibly successful Switch console. However, not all news from the future console front is without its shadows. While the full implications are yet to be seen, such news hints at potential challenges for hardware security that Nintendo may need to address down the line.

Overall, the combination of strong financial performance, overwhelming analyst confidence, and a pipeline of anticipated product announcements and console speculation paints a picture of a company poised for continued success. For both dedicated players and keen investors, Nintendo's next moves will be worth watching closely.
