Why Nintendo Stock Just Got a Power-Up

Recent analyst recommendations, upcoming events, and strong financials are painting a bullish picture for the gaming titan.

Across the Pacific, the Tokyo Stock Exchange (TYO: 7974) saw Nintendo’s stock price hit 7,294.0 JPY, marking a 3.02% increase in just 24 hours.

This collaboration taps into one of Nintendo's most beloved series, promising fresh merchandise for fans and new revenue streams for the company.
