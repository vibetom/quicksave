ASUS Just Dropped a Controller That Could Change Your Game

The new ROG Raikiri II Pro boasts 8K polling, hot-swappable joysticks, and more customization than you can shake a stick at. Is it the ultimate PC controller?

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jul 11, 2026 · 09:56 UTC 3 MIN

ASUS Republic of Gamers has officially launched the ROG Raikiri II Pro PC controller, a new piece of hardware that looks ready to make waves in the high-end PC gaming scene. Announced on July 9, 2026, this controller arrives packed with features designed to give competitive players a noticeable edge, especially those who demand pinpoint accuracy and lightning-fast reactions.

At its heart, the Raikiri II Pro is engineered for speed. It features a competition-grade 8K polling rate, a statistic that speaks to how quickly the controller can communicate with your PC. This high polling rate, coupled with ROG SpeedNova technology for its 2.4GHz wireless connection, promises incredibly responsive control and ultra-low latency. Think of it as a direct line from your brain to the game, with minimal static along the way.

But speed isn't the only play here. ASUS has also invested heavily in customization. The standout feature is undoubtedly the hot-swappable TMR joystick modules, which are designed to combat drift, a common enemy of longevity in controllers. Players aren't just stuck with one feel, either; the controller ships with two sets of joystick modules u2013 a default 120gf set and an additional 50gf set u2013 allowing gamers to fine-tune the actuation force to their exact preference. Every button, from the D-Pad to the ABXY keys and even the rear buttons and bumpers, uses micro switches for instant feedback and a satisfying tactile click.

Adding another layer of adaptability are the Dual Mode Triggers. This innovative design lets players switch seamlessly between short-travel micro-switch triggers, ideal for rapid-fire actions, and full-travel TMR sensor triggers for more nuanced control, like precise acceleration in a racing game. For those moments when sheer output is needed, a Turbo mode enables rapid auto-repeat with a single button hold. It's about giving players the right tool for every moment in the game.

The convenience factor hasn't been overlooked. The Raikiri II Pro offers an impressive power reserve, delivering up to 79 hours of wireless gameplay over its 2.4GHz connection from a single charge. For on-the-fly adjustments, a built-in full-color panel is integrated directly into the controller, making quick tweaks easy without breaking game immersion. For deeper customization, including button remapping and joystick sensitivity adjustments, the ASUS Gear Link software provides a comprehensive suite of options. From top to bottom, the ROG Raikiri II Pro is positioned as a premium, competition-grade device, meticulously crafted for players who demand a tailor-made, high-performance PC gaming experience.
