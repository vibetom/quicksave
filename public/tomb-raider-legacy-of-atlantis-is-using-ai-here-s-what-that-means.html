Tomb Raider: Legacy of Atlantis Is Using AI. Here's What That Means.

Crystal Dynamics says GenAI helps them get 'the right answers faster,' but early reports point to an 'awkward marriage' of old and new.

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jun 11, 2026 · 20:51 UTC 2 MIN

Crystal Dynamics is charting a new course for Lara Croft with Tomb Raider: Legacy of Atlantis, and part of that journey includes the integration of Generative AI (GenAI) tools into its development pipeline. This move, confirmed by Crystal Dynamics and reported by Wccftech on June 11, 2026, aims to "Get the Right Answers Faster" as the team crafts what is described as a "vivid, high-pace remake of a classic."

The ambition to deliver a modern take on a beloved classic is clear. The game is poised to blend familiar adventure elements with contemporary flair, hoping to resonate with both long-time fans and newcomers alike. However, the path to a harmonious blend might be a tricky one to navigate.

Interestingly, the adoption of cutting-edge AI tools stands in contrast to the very human, very physical demands placed on the game's lead performer. Alix Wilton Regan, the new voice and motion-capture actor for Lara Croft, endured significant physical strain during production, confirmed by GameSpot. Reports indicate Regan dislocated her jaw and busted her arm, underscoring the intense, hands-on commitment behind bringing Lara to life, even as digital tools augment the creative process.

Yet, early hands-on impressions suggest this marriage of past and present, high-tech and human grit, may not be entirely seamless. Engadget's reporting describes the game as an "awkward marriage of past and present." This raises a compelling question for players: will the speed and efficiency offered by GenAI truly coalesce with the game's classic roots and the raw, physical performance from its lead actor? As development continues, the gaming world will be watching to see if Legacy of Atlantis can successfully bridge these two worlds, or if its ambitious blend might create a new kind of challenge for Lara Croft.
