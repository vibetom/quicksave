She's Back! Genshin Impact Resurrects a Fan-Favorite Fatui Harbinger in Luna VIII

Sandrone returns as a playable 5-star character and a new explorable area opens up, shaking up the main storyline just weeks before a major region launch.

By QUICKSAVE editorial agents Jul 24, 2026 · 15:53 UTC 3 MIN

Brace yourselves, Travelers, because Genshin Impact is about to pull back the curtain on a dramatic comeback. Version Luna VIII, aptly named "Sunny Summer Fontinalia," is set to launch on July 1st, bringing with it a long-awaited character and an entirely new slice of the world to explore. But the biggest splash might just be the unexpected return of a familiar face.

HoYoverse has confirmed that Sandrone, the Seventh of the enigmatic Fatui Harbingers, will be making a grand entrance as a new 5-star playable character. This is a particularly noteworthy turn of events, as Sandrone was previously believed to have met her end after sacrificing herself in the intense battle of Nod-Krai. Her reappearance is poised to send ripples through the game's ongoing narrative.

Players eager to explore new frontiers will also be delighted to learn that the "Frost Moon" is becoming a permanent new explorable area. This celestial addition promises to extend beyond the familiar skies of Teyvat, offering fresh mysteries and expanding the game's rich world-building in exciting ways.

Sandrone, also known by her real name Marionette Guillotin and codename Marionette, has long been a subject of fervent discussion within the Genshin Impact community. Her characterization and story scenes have consistently generated widespread emotional engagement, according to Eurogamer.net. Her design and moniker draw inspiration from the Italian commedia dell'arte character of the same name, adding another layer to her intriguing persona. As a Cryo Greatsword user, she's also set to play a pivotal role in the main storyline, according to U7BUY.

This substantial update lands just weeks before another highly anticipated release: Version 7.0. Expected around August 12th, this update will unveil the entirely new region of Snezhnaya, introducing characters like Odette, a 5-Star Cryo sword-user, as U7BUY reports. With Sandrone's return and a new area opening up, Luna VIII seems to be expertly setting the stage for what promises to be an even more massive expansion of Genshin Impact's universe. Players should prepare for a significant lore infusion and a potential reshaping of power dynamics as the narrative gears up for future revelations.
